Intense Pulsed Light

Experience the magic of Intense Pulsed Light (IPL) therapy, a quick and efficient procedure that revitalizes your skin, leaving you with a refreshed, youthful appearance and minimal recovery time. IPL works its wonders by triggering the production of fresh collagen, resulting in the reduction of fine lines, diminished pores, and a smoother texture, while also fading acne scars. Additionally, IPL effectively reduces facial redness associated with conditions like Rosacea, erases unsightly brown spots, eliminates broken capillaries, and refines uneven skin.

Procedure

  • Contact us today to schedule a complimentary consultation.

  • During your consultation, we'll discuss your skin type and specific issues.

  • IPL Therapy involves a treatment series typically ranging from four to six sessions, during which a powerful burst of light is emitted, akin to a camera flash.

  • The intense pulsed light (IPL) penetrates beyond the surface layer of the skin, reaching the deeper layers where it targets blood vessels, pigment cells, and collagen.

  • One key advantage of IPL is that it leaves the epidermis intact, sidestepping the severe redness and peeling often associated with conventional laser skin resurfacing methods.

  • The duration of the procedure may differ based on the treated area's size.

  • IPL therapy for the entire face typically requires about 20 minutes, whereas treating the face, neck, and chest together may extend the session to approximately 45 minutes.

After Care

  • Shield your treated skin from direct sunlight for 48 hours and use SPF 30+ sunscreen when outdoors.

  • Avoid hot baths, harsh cleansers, and abrasive products for 48 hours post-treatment.

  • Keep skin hydrated with a gentle moisturizer, and stay well-hydrated from within by drinking water.

  • Skip makeup for at least 24 hours, then use gentle, mineral-based products.

  • Don't pluck, wax, or thread treated areas for two weeks.

  • Schedule and attend any recommended follow-up sessions for optimal results.
