Eyelash Extensions

Experience the magic of eyelash extensions. Our premium synthetic fibers are crimp-resistant, lightweight, and flexible, offering a weightless and comfortable feel. These extensions mimic natural lashes, available in various colors to match your style. Elevate your gaze with our expert treatments.

After Care

  • Refrain from using oily products, mascara removers, lash curlers, lash perms, or waterproof mascara on your extensions.

  • Minimize contact with natural skin oils, and avoid rubbing, scratching, or pulling your lashes. These actions can reduce the lifespan of your extensions.

  • Keep in mind that your natural lashes have a lifespan of approximately 100 days. As they naturally shed, new eyelashes grow in their place.

  • To preserve the beauty of your extensions, we recommend periodic re-lash appointments.
