FAQ

One of the primary benefits is the time saved each day. You no longer need to spend precious minutes applying makeup to your brows, lips, and eyeliner. Your features will already look defined and polished when you wake up.

Cosmetic tattoos provide a consistent look. Your eyebrows, lipliner, and eyeliner will maintain their shape and color, eliminating the need for constant touch-ups throughout the day.

Cosmetic tattoos can improve the appearance of sparse eyebrows, uneven lip lines, and lash lines. They can create the illusion of fuller brows, more defined lips, and thicker eyelashes.

Tattooed makeup can have a timeless quality, as it doesn't go out of style. You won't need to worry about keeping up with makeup trends.

Eyebrow, lipliner, and eyeliner tattoos are resistant to water and smudging. You can confidently go swimming, exercise, or just get caught in the rain without worrying about your makeup running.

Some people have allergies or sensitivities to traditional makeup products. Cosmetic tattoos eliminate the need for these products, reducing the risk of adverse reactions.

When performed by a skilled artist, cosmetic tattoos can look very natural. They enhance your facial features without appearing overly dramatic or artificial.
